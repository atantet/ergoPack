Detailed Description

Numerical stochastic model class. A modelStochastic is defined by a vector field, a stochastic vector field and a numerical scheme. The current state of the modelStochastic is also recorded. Attention: the constructors do not copy the vector field and the numerical scheme given to them, so that any modification or freeing will affect the modelStochastic.
